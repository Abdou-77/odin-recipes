Sheet Pan Parmesan Chicken and Veggies

This extra-crispy sheet pan chicken and veggies dinner is baked on one pan and packed with flavor!


Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon seasoned salt, or to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • ½ pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 1 small red potato, diced
  • 1 sweet bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup broccoli florets, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts, pounded flat and excess fat removed

Dipping Sauce (Optienal):

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise (Optional)
  • 1/4 cup ketchup (Optional)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (Optional)
  • 1/4 tsp Worcestershire sauce (Optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat the Oven and Prepare Sheet Pan
    • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare Seasoning Mix
    • In a small bowl, combine oregano, parsley, paprika, garlic powder, seasoned salt, and pepper.
  3. Prepare Vegetables
    • Place green beans, potatoes, bell peppers, broccoli, and garlic on the prepared sheet pan.
    • Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with half of the seasoning mix.
    • Toss to coat evenly.
  4. Prepare Breading Components
    • Place flour in one bowl.
    • Place melted butter in a second bowl.
    • In a third bowl, mix panko, 2/3 cup Parmesan cheese, and the remaining seasoning mix.
  5. Prepare and Bread Chicken
    • Slice chicken into 1 1/4-inch strips.
    • Coat chicken strips in flour.
    • Dredge floured strips in melted butter, allowing excess to drip off.
    • Press chicken into Parmesan-panko mixture until well coated on both sides.
  6. Assemble and Bake
    • Push vegetables to one side of the sheet pan.
    • Place coated chicken strips on the other side.
    • Sprinkle any remaining Parmesan-panko mixture over chicken, pressing gently to adhere.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
    • Flip chicken strips and stir vegetables.
    • Continue baking until chicken is cooked through (internal temperature of 165°F or 74°C) and vegetables are tender-crisp, about 10-15 minutes more.
  7. Prepare Dipping Sauce
    • While the chicken and vegetables bake, whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, garlic powder, and Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl.
  8. Finish and Serve
    • Remove chicken and vegetables from the oven.
    • Toss vegetables with remaining 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese.
    • Serve chicken and vegetables with the prepared dipping sauce.
