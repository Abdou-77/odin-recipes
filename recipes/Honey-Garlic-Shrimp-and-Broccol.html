Honey Garlic Shrimp and Broccoli

This quick and easy honey garlic shrimp and broccoli recipe is a perfect weeknight dinner, bursting with sweet and savory flavors!


Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • Cooked rice for serving

Directions

  1. Prepare the Honey Garlic Sauce
    • In a small bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, and sesame oil (if using).
  2. Cook the Shrimp
    • Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
    • Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and opaque.
    • Remove shrimp from the pan and set aside.
  3. Cook the Broccoli
    • In the same pan, add broccoli florets and cook for 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    • If needed, add a splash of water to steam the broccoli until tender.
  4. Combine Shrimp and Sauce
    • Return the shrimp to the pan with the broccoli.
    • Pour the honey garlic sauce over the shrimp and broccoli.
    • Stir well and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to combine flavors.
    • If a thicker sauce is desired, mix cornstarch with 1 tablespoon of water and stir into the pan.
  5. Serve
    • Remove from heat and serve immediately over cooked rice.
    • Drizzle with extra sesame oil if desired.
